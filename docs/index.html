HOW IT WORKS

A Seamless Workflow from Vision to Action

Our platform integrates perception, planning, and execution into a cohesive, intelligent system that operates with unmatched efficiency. Follow the three core stages of every task.

1. Scan & Identify

The system's 3D cameras map the workspace. Our neural network identifies and localizes every object, from beakers to centrifuges.

2. Plan & Optimize

Based on the goal, the motion planning algorithm computes the most efficient, collision-free trajectory for the arm and gripper.

3. Execute & Report

The UR3e arm executes the planned motion with precision. Every action is logged, and a report is generated upon task completion.
